This is a mini project for me to practice the vocabulary part of my SAT study process.

This works due to I created a google sheet of unknown words I have undergone.
Generally, it randoms 5 words along with detailed definition, then it blinds the info for me to remember (Active Recall Strategy).
The access is from the Google Cloud Service Account to get the information from the Google Sheet (as a hidden file secret.json and pass.txt)

Of course as one of my novel project so some factors are AI-related.
